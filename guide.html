How to order

  1. 1 Browse the menu and tap Add to put items in your cart.
  2. 2 Open your cart and tap Checkout. Sign in or create an account to order.
  3. 3 Choose pickup or delivery, enter a promo code if you have one, then place your order.
  4. 4 Track your order under My orders.
  5. 5 Tip: install the app to your home screen.
